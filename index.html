A registered private institution located in the serene environment of Lokotiye community. Open Day Academy is a technologically driven institution with state of the art facilities to enable students have sound educational foundation.

Embark on a journey of discovery and growth at Open Day Academy. From Creche to Primary, our programs are crafted to nurture curiosity, creativity, and a love for learning in every child. Rooted in safety, personalized attention, and holistic development, we inspire confidence, compassion, and resilience, shaping tomorrow's leaders with integrity and purpose. Join us in cultivating a brighter future, where every child's potential is celebrated and empowered.

Creche

Welcome to Open Day Academy's Creche Program, offering a nurturing environment for infants and toddlers. Our experienced caregivers prioritize safety, comfort, and early development, providing personalized attention and gentle guidance to support cognitive, emotional, and physical growth during these foundational years.

Kindergarten

Our Kindergarten Program sparks curiosity, creativity, and a love for learning in children. Through engaging activities and social interaction, we cultivate a stimulating environment where children explore, experiment, and develop essential skills, fostering independence, social skills, and a sense of community.

Nursery

In our Nursery Program, children aged 3 to 4 years embark on an enriching journey, preparing for primary education with structured learning and hands-on experiences. Our curriculum promotes language development, mathematical concepts, creative expression, and social-emotional skills, empowering each child to thrive academically, socially, and emotionally as they begin their educational journey.

Primary

Open Day Academy's Primary Program ignites a passion for learning and academic success in children aged 4 to 11 years. Our comprehensive curriculum integrates core subjects with experiential learning, critical thinking, and character development, nurturing confident, independent learners and responsible global citizens. With a focus on academic excellence, creativity, and holistic growth, we prepare students for success in school and beyond.
